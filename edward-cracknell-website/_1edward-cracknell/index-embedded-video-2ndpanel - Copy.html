What I Do

I compete in outdoor freestyle skydiving and wind tunnel competitions on a world class level.

By working for iFLY Manchester I get to develop and improve my bodyflight technique every day.

I am keen to pass on this knowledge by Freefly and Tracking coaching as well as Load Organising.

With NOVA we do regular trips to Empuriabrava to train with and learn from our coach, the one and only Will Penny.

Last year we became the UK National Champions and took 4th in the World in in Outdoor Freestyle Skydiving.

Watch this space as 2021 will be massive!

NOVA

2016-Present

NOVA is an outdoor freestyle skydiving team consisting of Ed Cracknell (performer) and Chris Mayhew (Video). With the help of our coach we have stepped up to make a name for ourselves on the international stage.
2021 will be really important to us, we have so much planned - including the UK Nationals and the World Championships.

Achievements so far:

2019 Skydiving World Cup: Freestyle, 4th Position - NOVA
2019 UK National: Freestyle A, Gold medalist - NOVA
2019 UK Indoor National: Freestyle, Silver medalist - Ed Cracknell
2018 UK National: Freestyle A, Bronze medalist - NOVA
2018 UK Indoor National: 4 Way Dynamic, Silver medalist - Ed Cracknell
2017 UK National: Freestyle A, Bronze medalist - NOVA
2016 UK National: Freestyle B, British Champion - NOVA
